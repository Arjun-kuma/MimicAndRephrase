Instructions

This tasks involves some creativity. Please read the instructions carefully. Expect to spend around 3min per HIT

  • Imagine you are asking for help from a company's customer service. The company could be anything (retail, insurance, shipping) be creative!
  • You will be creating four queries that may be asked of a customer service agent.
  • These must be long questions or requests. A minimum of 15 words is required.
  • Be creative, mix up the type of responses you give, have fun!
Example of GOOD responses: (Please read)

    Responses:

    1. I won't be able to pick up my order until tomorrow, but I scheduled it for pickup today. Can we change this?
    2. I need to put a lot of new info into my account, can you do it for me or do I have to type it all in online?
    3. When an employer hires a remote employee, how should the employer complete the Form I-9?
    4. I am considering a flight to New York on the 30th of August and would like to know what the cheapest airfair will be for that day if that's possible.

     

    Why these are GOOD responses:

    1. They are of a varying format and structure.
    2. They are creative and each address a unique question/task that would be asked of a customer service agent.
    3. They are all at least 15 words long.

Example of BAD responses: (Please read)

  1. Responses: (REMEMBER THESE ARE BAD!)

    1. Check on my order.
    2. Please check on my order.
    3. Check on my account.
    4. Hey, how are you? Glad to finally connect on this line, just have a few questions.

     

    Why these are BAD responses:

    • (1) - (3) are not bad on their own but are just slight rewordings of each other, all have nearly exactly the same format/meaning, and show no variety or creativity and are not long enough. We expect you to provide us with four unique and varying customer service requests that are at least 15 words long.
    • While (4) is a unique question and long enough, it is just small talk and does not represent a task/question that would be asked of a customer service agent.
